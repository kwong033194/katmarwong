Raised in Hawai'i, Kathleen is a bonafide beach-goer who suffers from a severe case of island fever. She graduated a year early from New York University with a double major in sociology and journalism. As an avid traveler, she has lived in Paris, France and Accra, Ghana, also exploring their surrounding areas. As a journalist, she seeks to discover compelling stories through the lens of lifestyle, whether it be emerging trends or breaking news. Kathleen has a wide range of editorial experience, having worked with Seventeen Magazine, High Times, Refinery29 and Mashable. Find her wandering at the MoMA.
