Kathleen Wong

Raised in Hawai'i, Kathleen is a bonafide beach-goer who suffers from a severe case of island fever. She graduated a year early from New York University with a double major in sociology and journalism. Kathleen has worked with Seventeen Magazine, High Times, Refinery29 and Mashable. As a journalist, she seeks to discover what her generation and this world are up to through a lens of lifestyle. Find her wandering at the MoMA.
